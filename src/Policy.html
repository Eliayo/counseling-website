Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy outlines our obligations and your rights concerning the collection and storage of your personal information when you use our website: www.integracounselling.co.uk.

Why Do We Process Your Information?

We process your personal data to fulfill our therapeutic obligations as outlined in the therapeutic contract. Your personal information enables us to contact you, discuss the therapeutic contract, and provide effective therapy. Additionally, your data may be used to improve our services. We are committed to ethically processing your data in compliance with legal obligations.

What Laws Protect Your Data?

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of 2019, any organization processing personal data is obligated to ensure that such data is:

  • Processed lawfully and collected for specific purposes
  • Accurate, necessary, and kept up-to-date
  • Stored only for as long as necessary and in an adequate amount
Our portal, www.integracounselling.co.uk, processes all data in compliance with GDPR (2019).

How Do We Collect and Treat Your Personal Information?

Your data is collected to fulfill our counselling obligations. We collect data through:

  • Our website: www.integracounselling.co.uk
  • Written communications
  • Phone calls
  • Counselling sessions

Integra Therapeutic Counselling processes your data according to GDPR (2019) guidelines.

How Long and How Will Your Data Be Stored?

Your data will be stored in both electronic and physical formats:

  • Electronic Format: Stored on password-protected devices and files, accessible only to authorized personnel. Names and contact details are stored separately from other personal data.
  • Physical Format: Stored anonymously in a locked cabinet, accessible only to authorized personnel.
In compliance with GDPR (2019), your data will not be stored for longer than necessary.

What Types of Information Are Collected on Our Website?

Our website collects different types of data, including:

  • Browsing Information: When you visit www.integracounselling.co.uk, we collect data such as your IP address, location, browser, date, time, and device.
  • Contact Form Information: If you fill out our contact form, we collect your name, phone number, date, and time.

We may also ask for your contact details and availability before signing the counselling contract.

Cookies

When you fill out the contact form, you can choose to save your name and email address. These cookies expire after one year, allowing your details to be auto-filled during your next visit.

What Are Your Rights Regarding Your Personal Data?

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request access to your data stored by our portal, www.integracounselling.co.uk.
  • Delete Your Data: Request the deletion of your data, except for information we are obligated to store for administrative, legal, or security reasons.

To access or delete your data, please contact us at anna@integracounselling.co.uk.
